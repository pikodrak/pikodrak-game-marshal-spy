HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Capture the enemy citadel with a ground unit. First player to do so wins.

THE BOARD

Diamond-shaped hexagonal grid with 153 hexes. Each player has:

CITADEL Your base. Defend it!
LEVEL 0 Defensive zone. +0 attack bonus.
LEVEL 1 Mid zone. +1 attack bonus.
LEVEL 2 Forward zone. +2 attack bonus.
BATTLEFIELD Neutral ground. Mountains block ground movement.

Your territory is always at the bottom, enemy at the top.

DEPLOYMENT PHASE

Place all 23 units into your Level zones (not citadel, not battlefield).

  • Click a unit in the side panel, then click a hex to place it
  • Or use RANDOM DEPLOY to auto-place everything
  • Higher levels give bigger attack bonus but have unit limits
  • Max 1 Assassin and 1 Terminator per level
  • Artillery can only go to Level 0

BATTLE PHASE

Players alternate turns. Each turn: one action — Move, Attack, Special, or Pass.

  • Green hexes — valid movement targets
  • Red hexes — attack targets
  • Blue hexes — special ability targets

COMBAT

  • Melee: Higher attack wins. Equal = both die. Winner moves to target hex.
  • Ranged: If attacker ≥ target: kill. If less: miss. Attacker always safe.
  • Helicopter vs Ground: Helicopter attacks without risk. Can't kill stronger, but survives.
  • Assassin: Attack 8 — devastating. But dies to ANY ground unit when defending!
  • Mine: Ground unit walks on it = both destroyed. Engineer defuses safely.

FOG OF WAR

  • All units start hidden — enemy sees position but not type
  • Units revealed during combat (both sides)
  • Jammer prevents reveals in its area
  • Recon Drone reveals enemies at range
  • Engineer can re-hide revealed allies

STRATEGY TIPS

  • Level 2 gives +2 attack but limits specials — plan your placement!
  • Scout with Recon before attacking — know what you're fighting
  • Protect Assassin — attack 10 at L2, but dies to anything on defense
  • Jammer + army — enemy can't scout your hidden units
  • Mines near citadel stop enemy rushes
  • Trainer boost key units before battle — permanent +1 each time
  • Rush: All combat units at L2, push fast before enemy sets up
  • Turtle: Mines, artillery, jammer at L0, strong defenders near citadel
