    • 2026-04-21 – v1.0 (WIP) – Complete ruleset overhaul (engine + AI + API skeleton)

    Breaking ruleset changes (v0.x saves rejected)

  • Assassin removed; Hacker added (auto-kills Terminator in melee)
  • Mine replaced by Minové pole – destroys any ground attacker except Engineer
  • Attack drone: threshold-kill (ATK < 4); miss = drone revealed + target temp-revealed
  • Artillery now acts via special action (range 2, kills ground/special)
  • Fighter: air only; non-air attacks → wasted turn (Zmařený tah)
  • Helicopter movement 1, attacks ground/air/special
  • Terminator ATK 8+level; Engineer/Hacker no level scaling
  • Trainer: boost +1 ATK max 2× per target; may convert ATK>3 ground into Hacker (2×/game)
  • Corruptor: -1 ATK or range (floor 1; wasted if at floor); no reveals on either side
  • Ranges: recon/attack drone/corruptor/jammer = 1+level; trainer 1; artillery 2
  • Placement: all hexes must be filled; special cap 2+level per level; 1 Terminator/level
  • Unit counts are player-chosen maxima (Ženista 8, Vojín 10, Průzkumník 6, Výsadkář 5, Tank 4, Terminátor 1, Hacker 1, Vrtulník 4, Stíhač 4, Minové pole 8)
  • First battle turn goes to whoever confirmed placement first
  • Fighter may path through own units (can't stop); no pathing through enemies
  • Citadel capture allowed by ground and special units (air still excluded)
  • Inactivity flow: 2-min idle → opponent may request Zrychlení hry → 60-sec force-pass

    • Engine / internals

  • Engine version bumped to 1.0; old v0.x saves rejected cleanly
  • Units gain boost_count, corrupt_attack, corrupt_range, temp_revealed_ticks_left, converted_from
  • Every action recorded in GameState.replay_actions for future replay UI
  • New engine API: place_new_unit, clear_placement, apply_preset, confirm_placement(force), request_speedup, check_speedup_expired, force_end_placement

    • AI

  • AI rewritten for new ruleset; placement fills all 51 hexes with balanced composition
  • Scoring tuned for hacker/terminator, drone threshold, artillery, fighter, corruptor
